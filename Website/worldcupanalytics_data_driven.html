Business Takeaways

What This Means For Media And Strategy

The strongest recruiter-facing value in this project is not just finding audience patterns. It is turning those patterns into recommendations for broadcasters, sponsors, and tournament operators.

Audience
Decision Makers
For Broadcasters
Price premium windows differently Host-nation matches, opening fixtures and knockout drama consistently over-index on audience demand, so they should be treated as premium inventory rather than as ordinary schedule fill.
Use the simulator for sales logic The 2026 model helps identify where star power, team quality and hosting effects justify higher expectations for English-language and Spanish-language reach.
For Advertisers
Concentrate budget on tentpoles A relatively small share of matches behaves like global tentpole inventory, so concentrated spend around peak windows is likely more efficient than evenly spreading investment.
Align creative with audience mode Group-stage inventory behaves like broad reach media, while knockout windows behave more like high-attention event television with stronger emotional intensity.
For Tournament Planners
Expansion adds volume, not equal value The 2026 format increases total audience opportunity, but the dashboard shows that premium value still concentrates in specific matches, stages and host-market moments.
Host-market exposure is commercially meaningful Host-team matches produce the strongest repeatable audience premium in the file, which supports giving host-market pathways and time windows special commercial attention.

Project Framing

About The Dataset, Tools And Business Lens

This block makes the project recruiter-readable in under a minute: what the dataset is, what stack was used, and what business questions the dashboard is actually trying to answer.

Positioning
BI Product
About Dataset
Coverage 256 World Cup matches across 2010, 2014, 2018 and 2022, plus a 2026 forecasting layer for scenario planning.
Cleaning Work Attendance-capacity issues were corrected, fill rates were recomputed, final flags were repaired, and quality markers were added before the dashboard was pointed at the cleaned file.
Hybrid Nature The file combines raw-style match metadata with engineered and modeled audience fields, so the dashboard is framed as a business analytics product rather than a pure official-statistics archive.
